FAQ.

FAQ.

Dental Lab FAQs

Common questions about case turn around times, billing, digital submissions, and how we partner with dental practices

What is the typical turnaround time for cases?

Our standard turnaround time varies depending on the restoration type. Most crown and bridge cases are completed within 3–5 business days.

Do you accept digital scans?

Yes. We accept digital impressions from most major intraoral scanners. Digital submissions help streamline the workflow, improve accuracy, and reduce turnaround times.

What types of restorations do you offer?

We provide a wide range of restorations including crowns, bridges, veneers, implant restorations, All-on-X solutions, dentures, and cosmetic restorations.

How do I submit a case to your lab?

Cases can be submitted either digitally through supported scanning systems or by sending traditional impressions via shipping.

Do your restorations come with a warranty?

Yes. We stand behind the quality of our work and offer a limited warranty on most restorations. Specific warranty terms may vary depending on the type of restoration and materials used.
